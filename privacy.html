Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4/11/2024

We appreciate you choosing Global Share Bot. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we gather, the way we use it, and the measures we take to secure your data. By using Global Share, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. Your trust is essential to us, and we are committed to maintaining your privacy with transparency.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

While using Global Share, we may collect non-personal information, such as the name and description of the server, as well as additional details provided during bot installation. This data is primarily used to enhance our services, improve user experience, and for analytics purposes to optimize performance. We may also monitor server interaction patterns to identify trends and improve user engagement.

In certain cases, we may collect user feedback and interactions for better service insights. This information is anonymized and helps in making service adjustments. For example, we may use survey data to tailor new features or address specific user requests.

2. Data Protection

We take the necessary precautions to protect the non-personal information received through Global Share, utilizing security protocols like encryption and access controls. Although we implement robust security measures, we cannot guarantee the absolute safety of your data, as no online communication or storage method is entirely risk-free. We advise users to maintain updated security practices for their servers as an additional safety measure.

Our team regularly reviews security practices to ensure the highest level of protection against potential breaches and unauthorized access. We also provide training to our staff to keep them informed about the latest data security standards.

3. Sharing of Information

Global Share will not share any user data with third parties without your explicit consent. We may use non-personal data for service improvements, but we will not disclose it to external parties without permission. In the event of legal requirements or court orders, we may disclose information as required by law. Users will be notified if such a situation arises, whenever possible and permitted by law.

4. Use of Cookies and Tracking

Global Share does not utilize cookies or any other tracking technologies to gather user data. We focus on maintaining a simple, non-intrusive user experience. If we ever introduce cookies or tracking tools, users will be informed, and consent will be requested beforehand.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain non-personal data until it is requested for deletion by the server owner or administrator. Such requests will be processed within thirty days. Upon deletion, data is permanently removed from our active databases and cannot be restored. For verification purposes, we may maintain minimal records of deletion requests.

We encourage server administrators to regularly review data and request deletions if necessary for maintaining privacy. This proactive approach helps to ensure data accuracy and relevancy.

6. Modifications to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any significant changes will be posted on this page, and users will be notified. Please check this policy periodically to stay updated on how we protect your information. Your continued use of Global Share following any modifications to this policy signifies your acceptance of the changes.

7. User Rights and Access

As a user, you have the right to access, modify, or request the deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the provided email address. We aim to respond to all data requests promptly and transparently. Users may also request information about data use practices or raise concerns regarding privacy, and we will address these as swiftly as possible.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at contactus.globalshare@gmail.com. Our team is committed to assisting you with any privacy-related issues or clarifications you may need.
